About Us

Welcome to Karwane Hasan

Founded in 2010 by Mr. Hasan Zaki Zaidi, KARWAN-E-HASAN began with a simple mission: to provide pilgrims with meaningful, safe, and spiritually enriching experiences as they visit the holiest sites in Islam.

With over a decade of experience, we have grown from a small family operation to one of the most trusted names in pilgrimage tours, serving thousands of satisfied pilgrims every year. Our deep understanding of the religious significance of these journeys, combined with our commitment to excellent service, has established us as leaders in organizing Ziyarat packages to Iraq, Iran, and Syria, as well as Hajj and Umrah services to Saudi Arabia.
